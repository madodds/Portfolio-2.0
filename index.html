Catching Numerators

Actionscript 3.0 - 2012

Teaching fractions while having fun. The player is given a decimal at the bottom of the screen that they have to match by catching numerators falling from the sky onto their assigned denominator. The denominator increases as the player levels up. Level ups happen when the player has achieved enough right answers relative to the amount of wrong answers. The max denominator is 10. When the 10th is complete the player is given a report card on their performance.
